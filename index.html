Offering the best of coastal and city living.

The Seychelle at Ocean Avenue South features beautifully designed interiors by world-renowned Clodagh Design International. Every detail celebrates serenity and simplicity, from the attended lobby with a concierge, to a residents-only rooftop pool and lounge. From your private balcony, take in dazzling vistas that make elegant cocktail parties and simple Sunday brunches equally unforgettable. Enjoy some of Los Angeles’ most cherished restaurants at your door step. The Seychelle is home to the Herringbone restaurant, while The Waverly is home to Joan’s on Third and eLOVate Cafe.

Restaurants Right at Your Doorstep

Residents at The Seychelle will soon be able to enjoy some of Los Angeles’ most cherished restaurants, right at their doorstep. Carefully selected for their warm atmosphere and commitment to excellence, these eateries will bring the Ocean Avenue South neighborhood together, inviting community and conversation. The Seychelle will soon be home to Herringbone restaurant, while The Waverly will soon be home to Joan’s on Third and eLOVate Café.

Joan’s On Third

Originally opened in 1998 as a catering company on Third Street in Los Angeles, Joan’s on Third blossomed into a Los Angeles culinary landmark. Family-owned and operated, the company offers an extensive gourmet marketplace, café and full-service catering and event planning company.

Herringbone

Welcome to the world of Herringbone, where every detail from the ‘fish meats field’ menu, to the décor and music, is filled with a sense of whimsy and fun. Whether you just come by for drink or stop in for dinner, you’ll quickly see why celebrity chef Brian Malarkey’s Herringbone isn’t just a restaurant, it’s an experience.

eLOVate

At eLOVate Café, chef Roberto Martin is committed to providing 100% organic, delicious vegan cuisine that is just as appealing to the non-vegan as the to the vegan. Guests will first be enticed by the beautiful café bar where they will dine and interact while watching mixologists create a phenomenal elixirs and smoothies. The dining patio will be anchored by a communal table, with outdoor lighting, heaters and awnings providing the perfect atmosphere for al fresco dining.
